Why Parsing Is Better

Baseline is fast and simple. Parsing is grammar-aware and usually more accurate on sentence flow.

Baseline

  • Keyword and length-based heuristics
  • Limited understanding of grammar roles
  • Can miss discourse relationships

Parsing-Based

  • Grammar-aware sentence analysis
  • Captures subject/verb/object relationships
  • Improved coherence and ordering quality
