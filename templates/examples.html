See sentence reordering in action

Real examples showing how shuffled sentences are restored using baseline and parsing-based methods.

Example 1

Shuffled Input

"said . The deal is on track , officials"

Baseline Output

"The deal is on track officials said"

Parsing Output

"The deal is on track," officials said.

Example 2

Shuffled Input

"after the update . markets reacted quickly"

Baseline Output

"markets reacted quickly after the update"

Parsing Output

"Markets reacted quickly after the update."

Example 3

Shuffled Input

"investors welcomed the decision , analysts said"

Baseline Output

"investors welcomed the decision analysts said"

Parsing Output

"Investors welcomed the decision," analysts said.

Example 4

Shuffled Input

"the agreement could be signed this week"

Baseline Output

"the agreement could be signed this week"

Parsing Output

"The agreement could be signed this week."
